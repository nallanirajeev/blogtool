Write for ThoughtNest

We welcome contributions from qualified writers and subject-matter experts. If you can write clearly, research thoroughly, and provide genuine value to readers — we'd love to hear from you.

What We're Looking For

  • Original, well-researched articles of at least 1,000 words
  • Practical, actionable content that genuinely helps readers
  • Clear, conversational writing — no academic jargon or keyword stuffing
  • Content not published or submitted elsewhere — we require exclusivity
  • No AI-generated or plagiarised content — we check

Topics We Accept

Technology (AI tools, software, cybersecurity, productivity), Health & Wellness (fitness, nutrition, mental health, sleep), Travel (India travel guides, budget travel, destination reviews), Personal Finance (investing, budgeting, saving, Indian tax tips), Lifestyle (home, books, productivity, wellbeing).

How to Submit

Email your pitch or draft to weareforyou959@gmail.com with subject line "Guest Post: [Your Topic]". Include a brief author bio and any previous writing samples. We respond to all pitches within 5 business days.
