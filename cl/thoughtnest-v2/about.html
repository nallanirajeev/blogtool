Why We Started ThoughtNest

ThoughtNest was started in 2022 by a group of journalists, educators, and professionals who were tired of two dominant content modes: shallow listicles optimised for clicks, and academic content too dense for everyday readers.

We believed there was a gap for writing that takes a topic seriously, researches it thoroughly, and presents it in plain language that respects the reader's intelligence. That's what ThoughtNest is.

We cover technology, health & wellness, travel, personal finance, and lifestyle — areas where good information genuinely improves lives, and where bad information causes real harm. We take that responsibility seriously.

Everything we publish is independently researched and written. We disclose all affiliate relationships clearly. We do not accept paid editorial placements. Our readers' trust is our only valuable asset.
